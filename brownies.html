Quick and Easy Brownies

Home

Description

This easy brownie recipe takes minutes to whip up in one bowl and about 20 minutes to bake for a moist and fudgy crowd-pleasing treat!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts (optional)

Steps

  • Mix the ingredients together in a bowl.
  • Spread the batter into a prepared brownie pan.
  • Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry and crinkled.
  • Cool completely, then cut into squares.
